About S S ENGINEERING

S S ENGINEERING is a Faridabad-based metalworking firm specialising in fabrication, mechanical press work, CNC machining, galvanisation and zinc plating, and high-quality paint finishes. We serve industrial and commercial customers with fast turnarounds and precision tolerances.

Our Mission

Deliver robust, precisely manufactured metal components with consistent surface finishes and on-time delivery.

Quality

Turnaround
